Empowering Communities Through Collective Action

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact across India.

Through education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, community development, and skill-building initiatives, we strive to create opportunities and improve lives.

✔ Section 8 Registered
✔ 80G & 12A Certified
✔ CSR-1 Registered
✔ NITI Aayog Registered
✔ ISO 9001:2015 Certified
